ABOUT US

Daffodils at a glance

Daffodils Public School is a co- educational, English Medium Public School. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) Delhi, up to the secondary stage. The school admits pupils of local as well as nearby regions and inculcation a spirit of secularism with respect for all religion.

Blossoming of Daffodils. The school was founded in 1997 as a middle school but today it has been developed into a full fledged senior secondary school including Arts, Commerce, Medical & Non-medical. The school is far off from the hustle-bustle of the city.
