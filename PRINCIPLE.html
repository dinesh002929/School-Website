From The Principle

At the very outset my overwhelmed heart turns to thank almighty for bestowing us with an opportunity and resources to serve ‘His’ most beautiful creation -‘Child’. The visionary zeal of the school management has flowered the school from a very modest beginning to a full-fledged senior secondary level since its inception in 1997.

We can justifiably be proud of consistent growth in the years gone by. The academic results have been tremendous and in co-curricular activities also Daffodils has acquitted itself very well. Daffodilians have made a mark in mathematics and science Olympiads and brought laurels to school by winning gold and silver accolades.

Mrs.Bhavika Chopra
